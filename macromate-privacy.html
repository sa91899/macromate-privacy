MacroMate Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

MacroMate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how MacroMate handles your information.

Information We Collect

MacroMate is a local-first application. All data you enter — including food logs, meal entries, glucose readings, macro goals, and personal settings — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal health data on any external server.

Data Stored On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Food and meal log entries
  • Custom food items and recipes
  • Glucose and carb tolerance test readings
  • Daily macro and calorie goals
  • App preferences and settings

Subscription and Billing

MacroMate uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you purchase a subscription, RevenueCat processes your payment through Google Play. RevenueCat may collect certain anonymized data to manage your subscription status. Please review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy for more information.

Barcode Scanning

When you use the barcode scanning feature, MacroMate sends the scanned barcode number to Open Food Facts (openfoodfacts.org) to retrieve nutritional information. No personal information is transmitted — only the barcode number itself.

Camera Permission

MacroMate requests camera access solely for barcode scanning. Camera data is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

No Advertising

MacroMate does not display advertisements and does not share your data with advertising networks.

No Account Required

MacroMate does not require you to create an account. No email address, name, or personal identifying information is collected by MacroMate.

Children's Privacy

MacroMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

MacroMate is a nutritional tracking tool and is not intended to provide medical advice. Always consult your healthcare provider before making changes to your diet, especially if you have diabetes or other medical conditions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
